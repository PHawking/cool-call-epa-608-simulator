# EPA 608 Type 1 Questions

## Question 1

**Question**

A near-azeotropic refrigerant blend typically has:

**Answer choices**

A. A single saturation pressure and temperature with no glide
B. One high saturation pressure value
C. Separate bubble-point and dew-point values that are close together
D. Separate bubble-point and dew-point values with a large difference

**Correct Answer**

Separate bubble-point and dew-point values that are close together

**Explanation**

Near-azeotropic blends have bubble-point and dew-point values that are very close together, resulting in a small temperature glide.

## Question 2

**Question**

A zeotropic blend will contain:

**Answer choices**

A. One saturation pressure value
B. One high saturation pressure value
C. Two saturation pressure values that are close
D. Two saturation pressure values that have a big difference

**Correct Answer**

Two saturation pressure values that have a big difference

**Explanation**

Zeotropic blends have a larger difference between their saturation pressures because their components have different boiling points.
